    The armor has thicker plating compared to its predecessor, Mark 42. The Mark 43 armor features three colors: dark red, silver and gold. The colors are painted on the plates of the armor and each have specific areas and designations on the armors' pieces. The Mark 43's color design is mostly inverted from that of its predecessor, featuring more red plates than gold and the red having a darker hue.
